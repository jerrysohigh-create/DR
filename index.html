WEB2 Foundation (Summary)

Operating entities include a BVI holding company and a Hong Kong operating entity, with supporting IP and brand registration across multiple jurisdictions. Core hardware platform is MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 (MAG1), manufactured via partnership with FIH through EVT → DVT → PVT → production phases. Certifications include GMS, GSMA TAC, FCC, CE, CP65, Battery CCC / CB / UN38.3, Common Criteria EAL 6+.
