Project Snapshot

MAGNE.AI is a hardware-software integrated Web3 ecosystem, anchored by the Phone Gen1 device family and the MHA / MS2 dual-token economy. The platform combines consumer hardware (Dual OS phones, HD Wallet, device-side AI), a manufacturing and supply chain partnership with industry leader FIH, and an on-chain participation / staking architecture deployed on BNB Smart Chain.

WEB2 Foundation (Summary)

Operating entities include a BVI holding company and a Hong Kong operating entity, with supporting IP and brand registration across multiple jurisdictions. Core hardware platform is Phone Gen1, manufactured via partnership with FIH through EVT → DVT → PVT → production phases. Certifications include GMS, GSMA TAC, FCC, CE, CP65, Battery CCC / CB / UN38.3, Common Criteria EAL 6+.

WEB3 Infrastructure (Summary)

Token economy includes MHA (10,000,000,000 total supply) and MS2 (Season 2 activity token). Deployment begins on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20) with planned L1 / L2 expansion. Public participation through payment.magne.ai, with on-chain lottery and staking contracts deployed. Session 1 (1,000 Phone Gen1 units) and Session 2 (1,600 Phone Gen1 units) are the primary campaigns. MS2 ≠ MHA, no fixed exchange rate, no binding return promise.
