Use Case Framework

MHA is positioned across six functional areas in the MAGNE.AI ecosystem:

  • Device: MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 (MAG1) hardware participation incentives
  • AI Agent: AI Agent economy settlement and incentives
  • Network: MAGNE.AI network governance and value transfer
  • Staking: Staking rewards and eligibility qualification
  • Ecosystem: Ecosystem partners, liquidity incentives, Builder programs
  • Future Expansion: Cross-chain bridging and Layer 2 settlement

Eligible Allocation Pool: 35,000,000 MHA allocated to eligible MS2 stakers based on effective staking weight.
