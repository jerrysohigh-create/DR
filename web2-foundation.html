Product & Hardware

MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 (MAG1) (Current Production)

MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 (MAG1) is the flagship hardware platform, integrating a Dual OS architecture, embedded HD Wallet, and on-device AI capability. The device supports the MAGNE.AI participation ecosystem through an embedded wallet and a Private Space for crypto-native functionality.

MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 (MAG1) Key Specifications (Public Summary)

  • UNISOC T9100 5G
  • 8 TOPS NPU
  • Up to 12 GB RAM + 512 GB UFS 3.1
  • 6.72 inch FHD+ 120 Hz display
  • Android 15 + NFC
  • 5000 mAh + IP52
  • Google GMS Approved
  • Widevine L1 Supported
  • GSMA TAC Obtained
  • FCC Public
  • CB Completed
  • UN38.3 Passed
  • CP65 Passed
  • CE In Progress
