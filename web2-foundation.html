Product & Hardware

Phone Gen1 (Current Production)

Phone Gen1 is the flagship hardware platform, integrating a Dual OS architecture, embedded HD Wallet, and on-device AI capability. The device supports the MAGNE.AI participation ecosystem through an embedded wallet and a Private Space for crypto-native functionality.

  • Dual OS (Android + secure element)
  • Embedded HD Wallet (key management + transaction signing)
  • Device-side AI (large model demo on AX3596 NPU)
  • Cloud AI integration (Google Gemini3 demo)
  • Gaming performance optimization
