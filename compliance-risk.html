Compliance & Risk Disclosures

No Binding Return Promise · Regional Restrictions · Eligibility Review · Subject to Final Review

Last Updated: 2026-07-14

Read Carefully: This page contains critical risk disclosures relevant to MAGNE.AI's token ecosystem, hardware participation, and distribution activities. By reviewing this Data Room, you acknowledge the following terms.

1. No Binding Return Promise

MAGNE.AI's token ecosystem does not constitute a financial product, financial instrument, or guaranteed-yield program. There is no guarantee that any participant will receive a positive return on participation, staking, or any other activity.

2. No Fixed Interest Rate

Staking, mining, and activity-based allocations are subject to variable outcomes based on actual participation, eligibility review, and official rule application. No fixed interest rate is promised.

3. No Binding Listing Commitment

Any reference to exchange listing, market availability, or trading venues is subject to final exchange, regulatory, and compliance review. There is no guarantee that the MHA token or any other related token will be listed on any specific exchange.

4. No Binding Token Value

Token values are determined by market dynamics and may be subject to significant volatility. There is no guarantee of token value stability, appreciation, or liquidity.

5. No Investment Advice

This Data Room is for informational purposes only. Nothing in this Data Room constitutes investment advice, financial advice, legal advice, or a recommendation to participate in any token purchase, sale, or activity.

6. Regional Restrictions

MAGNE.AI's activities are subject to regional restrictions and applicable regulations in different jurisdictions. Participants are responsible for ensuring compliance with their local laws and regulations. Certain regions or jurisdictions may be excluded from participation.

7. KYC / AML Requirements

KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) verification may be required for certain activities, including staking rewards distribution and any allocation that requires identity verification. Failure to complete KYC/AML may result in disqualification from rewards or allocations.

8. Anti-Abuse and Sybil Checks

Self-referral, bot activity, wash trading, circular funds, addresses under common control, Sybil behavior, or other anomalous patterns may result in reward cancellation and eligibility disqualification. MAGNE.AI reserves the right to apply anti-abuse measures including but not limited to address blacklisting, reward forfeiture, and participant exclusion.

9. Final Distribution Subject to Eligibility Review

All distributions — including but not limited to MS2 activity credits, MHA eligible allocation records, staking rewards, lottery selections, and other activity-based allocations — are subject to final review including eligibility verification, anti-abuse checks, TGE timing, regional restrictions, and applicable regulations. Distribution timing, amounts, and form are subject to change.

10. Smart Contract and Deployment Items

Certain contract configuration items are under technical review and will be confirmed before production deployment. Contract addresses, network parameters, and token mechanics may be subject to updates. All technical implementations are subject to security review and audit.

11. Token, Campaign, and Listing Information

Token distribution schedules, campaign mechanics, and listing-related information are subject to final exchange, legal, and compliance review. Any forward-looking statements in this Data Room are subject to change without notice.

Public Sale OG Specific Disclosures

Public Sale OG information, including price level, lock-up, vesting schedule, purchase records, and MHA-denominated amounts, is provided for due diligence reference only and remains subject to internal sale records, treasury records, refund records, eligibility review, and compliance review. It does not constitute investment advice, a guaranteed listing outcome, or a guarantee of future market performance.

Token Distinction: MS2 vs MHA

Important: MS2 and MHA are distinct tokens. MS2 is a Season 2 activity token used for activity tracking, ranking, staking weight, and future eligibility review. MHA is the core MAGNE.AI token with broader ecosystem utility. There is no fixed exchange rate between MS2 and MHA. The two tokens are not interchangeable at any fixed rate, and the existence of one does not imply any value or right related to the other.

Anti-Abuse Specific Rules

The following specific anti-abuse measures apply to MAGNE.AI's participation and reward systems:

  • Single-level referral only: Referral rewards apply only to first-level direct referrals. Multi-level (second-tier, third-tier, etc.) rewards do not exist.
  • Fixed referral relationship: Once the referred user completes their first valid participation, the referral relationship is fixed and cannot be modified.
  • Official entry excluded: Participation via the official default entry does not generate referral rewards and does not count toward leaderboards.
  • Secondary market excluded: Secondary market MS2 purchases, trading volume, or external MS2 transfers do not generate direct referral rewards.
  • Large rewards subject to additional review: Large reward amounts, leaderboard qualifications, and prize pool distributions may require additional eligibility review, KYC/AML compliance, and applicable regional restrictions.
